privacy & AI coaching info

What Keypace analyzes

All typing analysis — WPM, accuracy, inter-key intervals, character error rates, bigram timing, hesitation patterns, and speed decay — is calculated locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device during analysis.

What is sent to the AI coach

After a test, a structured summary of your diagnostic metrics (e.g. "accuracy: 94%, slowest bigram: 'th' at 320ms, limiter: consistency") is sent to Anthropic's Claude API via a secure Cloudflare Worker. Your raw keystrokes, the text you typed, your email address, and your username are never sent to Anthropic.

Data storage

Test history and personal bests are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. If you create an account and sign in, your history and bests are also stored in a Supabase database associated with your email. Raw keystroke timestamps from your 50 most recent tests are kept locally for replay purposes and are not uploaded.

AI coaching limitations

AI coaching insights are generated by a language model and may be imperfect or occasionally imprecise. Keypace does not provide medical, ergonomic, or physical therapy advice. Coaching is educational guidance only.

Deleting your data

You can clear all local data by clearing your browser's site data for keypace.io. To delete your account and cloud data, sign in and use the sign-out option — or contact contact@keypace.io to request full deletion.
